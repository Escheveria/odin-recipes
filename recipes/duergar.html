Description

The Duergar (Gray Dwarves0 are the hated subterranean cousins of the surface dwelling Shield Dwarves. Vast Duergar kingdoms exist in the Underdark beneath Toril's surface. Duergar are known for their foul tempers, penchant for cruelty, grim and bitter dispositions. Their food is as coarse and uncouth as they are.

Clockwise from top left: Sour Deep rothé pepper cheese, boiled Deep rothé kidney, foraged roots and tubers; skirret and Fellroot, contorted strangler fungus, onion & mushroom gravy hand pies.

Ingredients

  • Limburger
  • Whole beef kidney
  • Ginger and tumeric roots
  • Enoki mushrooms
  • Morel mushroom with shallot and dill in cream sauce
